Professional Summary

Adeel Akhtar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. His research focuses on nonlinear control, geometric control, hybrid systems, cyber-physical systems, and mobile and aerial robotics.
