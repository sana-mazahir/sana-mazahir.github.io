## Teaching at NJIT
- Introduction to System Dynamics (ME-305)
- Course outline
  - Mathematical review: complex algebra, linear differential equations, and Laplace transforms
  - Inverse Laplace transforms and system response
  - Modeling of mechanical systems
  - Work, energy, and energy-based modeling methods
  - Block diagrams and transfer functions
  - Electromechanical systems
  - Transient response analysis and system response specifications
  - Impulse response and frequency-domain analysis
  - Frequency response, Bode plots, and vibration isolation
  - State-space modeling of dynamical systems
  - Introduction to control systems
  - Automatic controllers and controller design
  - Advanced topic: control of a UAV
  - Review

## Teaching at University of Waterloo, Fall 2018, Instructor
#### Autonomous Mobile Robotics (MTE-544)
- Upper year undergraduate full course
- Class consisting of almost 100 students
- Course outline
	- Introduction, Probability, State Space 
	- Coordinate Transforms, Motion Modeling  
	- Measurement Modeling, Estimation: Bayes Filter  
	- Estimation: Kalman Filter, Extended Kalman Filter 
	- Estimation: Particle Filter, Mapping: Localization
	- Mapping: Mapping, EKF SLAM
	- Mapping: Graph SLAM 
	- Mapping: Scan Registration 
	- Control: Linear, Nonlinear 
	- Planning: Motion Primitives, Graph Based 
	- Planning: Probabilistic, Optimal 
	- Special topics: Geometric controls 
- Three lab modules involving ROS and real robots (turtle-bots)
	- lab 1: mapping 
	- lab 2: localization
	- lab 3: SLAM
